Game Mission

This is the story of the Iron-Knight, whom found himself lost in the "Forest of Hack". His mission should he choose to live is to make it through the forest, but that is easier said than done. The forest is filled with evil spirits called "LABS" that he must destroy. Nobody ever said the journey would be easy, however you don't have to walk through it alone. Do you have the courage to help guide the Iron-Knight through the forest and unleash his full potential...?
